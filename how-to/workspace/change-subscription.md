# How to Change Your Subscription

Upgrade or downgrade your cobby subscription plan to match your business needs.

## Prerequisites

- Workspace owner access
- Active cobby subscription

## Steps

1. Log in to the cobby portal at [app.cobby.io](https://app.cobby.io).

2. Go to **Settings** â†’ **Billing Portal**.

3. Scroll to the **Change plan** section.

   ![Change Plan Section](/img/cheap-portal/pic6.png)

4. Click **Change plan**.

5. Select your new subscription plan from the available options.

   ![Select New Plan](/img/cheap-portal/pic7.png)

6. Review the changes and confirm.

**Result:** Your subscription plan will change at the end of your current billing period.

## Understanding Plan Changes

**Upgrades (to a higher tier)**
- Take effect immediately
- You will be charged a prorated amount for the current period
- New features become available right away

**Downgrades (to a lower tier)**
- Take effect at the end of the current billing period
- No immediate changes to your service
- You will not be charged until the next billing cycle

## Common Issues

**Change plan button disabled?**
- Changes can only be made at specific times in your billing cycle
- Contact support if you need to change immediately

**Need to change during trial period?**
- Plan changes during trial work differently
- Contact support@cobby.io for assistance

**What happens to my data when downgrading?**
- Your product data remains intact
- Some features may become unavailable based on plan limits
- Review plan limits before downgrading
