# How to Manage Billing

Access and manage your cobby billing information, payment methods, and invoices.

## Prerequisites

- Workspace owner access
- Active cobby subscription

## Access Billing Portal

1. Log in to the cobby portal at [app.cobby.io](https://app.cobby.io).

   ![Login Screen](/img/cheap-portal/pic1.png)

2. Go to **Settings** â†’ **Billing Portal**.

**Result:** The billing portal displays your subscription and payment information.

![Billing Portal Overview](/img/cheap-portal/pic2.png)

## Update Payment Method

**Note:** This only works for credit card payments. For SEPA direct debit changes, contact support.

1. In the billing portal, locate the **Payment method** section.

   ![Payment Method Section](/img/cheap-portal/pic3.png)

2. Click **Add payment method**.

3. Enter your new credit card details.

4. Save the changes.

**Result:** Your new payment method is saved and will be used for future charges.

## Update Billing Information

1. In the billing portal, locate the **Billing Information** section.

   ![Billing Information](/img/cheap-portal/pic4.png)

2. Update your email address, address, or tax ID as needed.

3. Save the changes.

**Result:** Your billing information is updated and will appear on future invoices.

## Download Invoices

1. In the billing portal, scroll to **Billing history**.

   ![Billing History](/img/cheap-portal/pic5.png)

2. Click the download icon next to the invoice you need.

**Result:** The invoice or payment receipt downloads to your computer.

## Common Issues

**Cannot change SEPA payment method?**
- SEPA direct debit changes must be made through support
- Contact support@cobby.io with your new account details

**Need to update your subscription plan?**
- See [How to Change Your Subscription](./change-subscription.md)

**Invoice missing or incorrect?**
- Check the billing history for all available invoices
- Contact support@cobby.io if you cannot find an expected invoice
