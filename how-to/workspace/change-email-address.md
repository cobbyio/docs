# How to Change Your Email Address

Update the email address associated with your cobby workspace account.

## Prerequisites

- Access to your new email address
- Workspace owner or admin access
- New email must be registered with cobby

## Steps

1. Log in to the cobby portal at [app.cobby.io](https://app.cobby.io).

2. Go to **Settings** â†’ **Users**.

   ![Settings Users Menu](/img/working-in-a-team/team_2.png)

3. Enter your new email address in the input field.

   ![Add New Email](/img/working-in-a-team/team_3.png)

4. Click **Add**.

**Result:** Your new email address appears in the user list.

![New Email Added](/img/working-in-a-team/team_5.png)

5. Remove your old email address by clicking the trash can icon next to it.

   ![Remove Old Email](/img/working-in-a-team/team_7.png)

6. Click **Remove** to confirm.

   ![Confirm Removal](/img/working-in-a-team/team_6.png)

**Result:** Your account now uses the new email address.

## Common Issues

**Error when adding new email?**
- Register the new email address at [app.cobby.io](https://app.cobby.io) first
- The email may already be in use in another workspace

**Need to transfer ownership?**
- If you have owner permissions, transfer ownership to the new email before removing the old one
- See [How to Manage User Permissions](./manage-permissions.md)

**Locked out of your account?**
- Contact support at support@cobby.io if you cannot access either email address
