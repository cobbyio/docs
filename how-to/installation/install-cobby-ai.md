# Install cobby AI

This guide shows you how to install the cobby AI Excel Add-in. Follow these steps if you want to use cobby AI features without a full shop integration.

## Prerequisites

- A Microsoft account
- Microsoft Excel (Microsoft 365)
- A cobby account (register at [app.cobby.io](https://app.cobby.io/login) if you don't have one)

---

## Step 1: Find the cobby App in the Microsoft Store

1. Go to [https://appsource.microsoft.com/de-de/product/office/WA200004128?tab=Overview](https://appsource.microsoft.com/de-de/product/office/WA200004128?tab=Overview)

2. Click **Jetzt abrufen** (Get it now)

---

## Step 2: Install the Excel Add-in

1. When prompted, log in with your **Microsoft account** credentials

2. Click **In Excel Ã¶ffnen** (Open in Excel)

:::tip
The add-in will now be available every time you open Excel on this computer.
:::

---

## Step 3: Open the cobby Task Pane

1. In Excel, look at the ribbon tabs at the top

2. Click on the **cobby PIM** tab

3. Click the button labeled **cobby PIM Task pane anzeigen** (Show cobby PIM task pane)

---

## Step 4: Log in to cobby AI

1. In the cobby task pane, click **Login**

2. Enter your cobby account credentials

3. You now have access to cobby AI features

:::tip Success Indicator
With the cobby task pane open and logged in, you're ready to use cobby AI to enhance your product data!
:::
